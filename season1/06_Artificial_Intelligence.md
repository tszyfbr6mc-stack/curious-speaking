# Final Speaking

Artificial intelligence learns patterns from data instead of memorizing facts, allowing it to make useful predictions. Data is important because AI learns from many examples rather than a single answer. AI compares examples again and again to find patterns that help it make decisions. People can benefit from AI most when they use it wisely while continuing to think critically.
