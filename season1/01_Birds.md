# Curious Speaking
## Founder's Edition
### Master Unit 01 — Birds (Revision Draft)

**Theme:** Nature  
**Driving Question:** Why do birds migrate?

---

# Pattern Training

## What

| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
| 1 | Some birds migrate. |
| 2 | Some birds migrate every autumn. |
| 3 | Some birds migrate every autumn to warmer regions. |
| 4 | Some birds migrate every autumn to warmer regions where food is easier to find. |

## Why

| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
| 1 | Birds need food. |
| 2 | Birds need food during winter. |
| 3 | Birds need food during winter because insects become scarce. |
| 4 | Birds need food during winter because insects become scarce in cold weather. |

## How

| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
| 1 | Many birds fly together. |
| 2 | Many birds fly together in a V shape. |
| 3 | Many birds fly together in a V shape to save energy. |
| 4 | Many birds fly together in a V shape to save energy on long journeys. |

## What if

| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
| 1 | Winters might become warmer. |
| 2 | Winters might become warmer in some regions. |
| 3 | Winters might become warmer in some regions because of climate change. |
| 4 | Winters might become warmer in some regions because of climate change, changing the way some birds migrate. |

# Automatic Response

1. Why do some birds migrate?  
→ They migrate to find food.

2. When do many birds migrate?  
→ They migrate every autumn.

3. Why do many birds fly in a V shape?  
→ To save energy.

4. How might climate change affect birds?  
→ It might change the way they migrate.

# Final Speaking

Some birds migrate every autumn to warmer regions where food is easier to find. They need food during winter because insects become scarce in cold weather. Many birds fly together in a V shape to save energy on long journeys. As the climate changes, the way some birds migrate may change in the future.
