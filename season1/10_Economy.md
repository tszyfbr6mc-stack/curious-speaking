# Automatic Response

1. Why do prices change?  
→ Because supply and demand change.

2. When do prices usually rise?  
→ When demand becomes higher than supply.

3. What helps balance markets?  
→ Prices help balance markets.

4. What might happen if demand falls?  
→ Sellers might lower their prices.

# Final Speaking

Prices change every day in markets as people buy and sell goods. When demand becomes higher than supply, prices usually rise. Markets balance supply and demand through prices that change as buyers and sellers make decisions. Every choice people make with their money influences the economy little by little.
