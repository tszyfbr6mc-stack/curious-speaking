# Curious Speaking
## Founder's Edition
### Master Unit 02 — Coral Reefs

**Theme:** Nature  
**Driving Question:** Why are coral reefs called the rainforests of the sea?

---

# Automatic Response

1. Why are coral reefs important?  
→ They provide food and shelter.

2. What builds a coral reef?  
→ Tiny coral animals build it.

3. How long does a coral reef take to grow?  
→ It grows over many years.

4. What might happen if coral reefs disappear?  
→ Many sea animals would lose their homes.

|1|Many fish return to coral reefs.|

---

# Final Speaking

Coral reefs are living ecosystems in warm oceans that support thousands of different sea animals. Many animals depend on coral reefs for shelter and food because young sea animals can grow there safely. Coral reefs grow slowly as tiny coral animals build them layer by layer. If coral reefs disappear because the oceans become warmer, many sea animals will lose their homes.
