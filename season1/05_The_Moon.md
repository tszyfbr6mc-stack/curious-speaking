## Founder's Edition
### Master Unit 05 — The Moon

**Theme:** Space  
**Driving Question:** Why does the Moon seem to change shape?

# Automatic Response

1. Why does the Moon shine?  
→ Because it reflects sunlight.

2. Does the Moon make its own light?  
→ No, it reflects the Sun's light.

3. How long does one orbit take?  
→ About one month.

4. What would happen without the Moon?  
→ The tides would change.

# Final Speaking

The Moon changes shape every month as it orbits Earth, creating different phases in the night sky. It reflects sunlight instead of making its own light, so we see different phases as its position changes. The Moon moves around Earth about once a month, changing the angle of sunlight we see. Without the Moon, the night sky and the ocean tides would be very different.
