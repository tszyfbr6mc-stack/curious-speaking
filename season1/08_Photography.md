# Curious Speaking
## Founder's Edition
### Master Unit 08 — Photography

**Theme:** Art & Memory
**Driving Question:** Why are photographs powerful?

# Pattern Training

## What
| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
|1|Photographs capture moments.|
|2|Photographs capture important moments.|
|3|Photographs capture important moments that quickly pass.|
|4|Photographs capture important moments that quickly pass, allowing us to remember them later.|

## Why
| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
|1|Memories fade.|
|2|Memories fade over time.|
|3|Memories fade over time unless we revisit them.|
|4|Memories fade over time unless we revisit them through stories or photographs.|

## How
| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
|1|A camera records light.|
|2|A camera records light through a lens.|
|3|A camera records light through a lens to create an image.|
|4|A camera records light through a lens to create an image that preserves a moment in time.|

## What if
| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
|1|Old photos might disappear.|
|2|Old photos might disappear forever.|
|3|Old photos might disappear forever if they are not protected.|
|4|Old photos might disappear forever if they are not protected, taking valuable family memories with them.|

| Word | IPA | Meaning |
|---|---|---|

| preserve | /prɪˈzɜːrv/ | 보존하다 |

# Automatic Response
1. What do photographs capture? → Important moments.
2. Why are photographs valuable? → They help preserve memories.
3. How does a camera work? → It records light through a lens.
4. What might happen to old photos? → They might disappear forever.

# Expansion Challenge
| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
|1|One picture tells a story.|
|2|One picture tells a meaningful story.|
|3|One picture tells a meaningful story without many words.|
|4|One picture tells a meaningful story without many words because images can express powerful emotions.|

# Final Speaking
Photographs capture important moments that quickly pass, allowing us to remember them later. Memories fade over time unless we revisit them through stories or photographs. A camera records light through a lens to create an image that preserves a moment in time. Protecting old photographs helps preserve family memories for future generations.
