# Curious Speaking
## Founder's Edition
### Master Unit 09 — The Internet

**Theme:** Technology & Society
**Driving Question:** How has the Internet changed communication?

# Pattern Training

## What
| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
|1|The Internet connects people.|
|2|The Internet connects people instantly.|
|3|The Internet connects people instantly across long distances.|
|4|The Internet connects people instantly across long distances through messages, videos, and shared information.|

## Why
| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
|1|Information spreads quickly.|
|2|Information spreads quickly online.|
|3|Information spreads quickly online because people share it.|
|4|Information spreads quickly online because millions of people share ideas every day.|

## How
| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
|1|People communicate online.|
|2|People communicate online in many ways.|
|3|People communicate online in many ways using different platforms.|
|4|People communicate online in many ways using different platforms to learn, work, and build relationships.|

## What if
| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
|1|The Internet might stop working.|
|2|The Internet might stop working for a day.|
|3|The Internet might stop working for a day in one country.|
|4|The Internet might stop working for a day in one country, changing how people communicate and work.|

# Automatic Response
1. What does the Internet do? → It connects people.
2. Why does information spread quickly? → Because people share it online.
3. How do people communicate? → They use different platforms.
4. What if the Internet stopped working? → Communication would become more difficult.

# Expansion Challenge
| Step | Sentence |
|---|---|
|1|Technology changes society.|
|2|Technology changes society every day.|
|3|Technology changes society every day by connecting people.|
|4|Technology changes society every day by connecting people and making information easier to share.|

# Final Speaking
The Internet connects people instantly across long distances through messages, videos, and shared information. Information spreads quickly online because millions of people share ideas every day. People communicate using different platforms to learn, work, and build relationships. If the Internet stopped working for a day, communication would become much more difficult.
